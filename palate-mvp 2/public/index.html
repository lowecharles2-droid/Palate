Transparent recommendations

What “authentic” means on Palate.

Palate scores cuisine fidelity: how clearly a restaurant delivers the food style it claims to represent. That is separate from whether it is locally owned.

A chain can faithfully deliver its category and still receive a low local score. Every listing includes the reasoning and source links behind its tags.

9–10Specialist menu with strong cuisine heritage, technique, or direct restaurant evidence.
7–8Clear category identity that may be modernized, adapted, or delivered by a larger chain.
5–6Broad menu where the named cuisine is present but not the main specialist focus.
