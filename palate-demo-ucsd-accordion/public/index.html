Focused LaunchX version

One killer feature: personalized local discovery.

Target user: college students and young professionals who want authentic, good-value local food near UCSD.

Core problem: Yelp, Google Maps, and TikTok can be cluttered, outdated, or hard to personalize. Palate tests whether people prefer curated, taste-based discovery.

Proof of concept: users pick goals like high protein, vegetarian, spicy, cheap, authentic, or hidden gems, then Palate ranks real nearby restaurants and groups them into collapsible sections.

🎯

Customer persona

UCSD students, nearby interns, and young professionals who eat out, use social media for food ideas, and care about value.

💡

Value proposition

Discover authentic local food that matches your taste, budget, distance, and nutrition goals — all in one place.

🧪

What to test

Do users care more about AI search, curated collections, authenticity score, distance, or nutrition filters?
