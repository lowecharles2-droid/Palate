## Accuracy limits

- Hours and operating status can change after verification.
- Approximate distance is for discovery only, not navigation.
- Dietary tags indicate that relevant choices appear on official descriptions or menus. They do not guarantee allergy safety, certification, or freedom from cross-contact.
- Palate does not display invented calorie or macronutrient totals.

## Cuisine-fidelity rubric

- 9–10: specialist menu with strong heritage, technique, family-recipe, or direct cuisine evidence
- 7–8: clear category identity but modernized, fusion-based, or chain-operated
- 5–6: broad or heavily adapted identity
- Local/independent status is scored separately
