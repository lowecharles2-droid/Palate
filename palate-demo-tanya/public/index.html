Blue ocean positioning

Not another Yelp. A curated food discovery platform.

People struggle to answer: “Where can I find authentic, good food that fits my budget and goals?”

Yelp can feel outdated, Google Maps is cluttered, TikTok is hard to organize, and delivery apps focus on ordering instead of discovery. Palate combines taste matching, curated collections, and a lightweight nutrition layer.

🎯

Target users

College students and young professionals who love trying local food, care about value, and discover restaurants through TikTok or Instagram.

⭐

Authenticity score
